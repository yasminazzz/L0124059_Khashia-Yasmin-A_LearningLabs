About Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is an American singer-songwriter (born December 13, 1989, West Reading, Pennsylvania, U.S.). Known for her storytelling and artistic reinventions, she is a leading figure in popular music, capturing hearts worldwide with her musical journey. Taylor Swift debuted with her self-titled album in October 2006. In 2024 she made history when she won the Grammy Award for album of the year for Midnights (2022), becoming the first artist to win in that category four times.
